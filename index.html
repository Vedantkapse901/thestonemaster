Raigad's Legacy of Stone

In the heart of Maharashtra, where the rugged Sahyadri mountains whisper tales of Maratha pride, we practice the ancient art of stone masonry. From intricate temple sanctums to modern architectural marvels, our chisels breathe life into the silent earth.

Rooted in Tradition

Founded in Raigad, The Stone Master began as a small family atelier dedicated to the preservation of traditional Indian stone carving techniques. Our founder, a master sculptor with over 40 years of experience, believed that stone is the only medium capable of capturing the true permanence of human emotion.

Today, we blend those ancient techniques with modern precision tools to create bespoke art pieces that stand the test of time, weather, and shifting trends.
